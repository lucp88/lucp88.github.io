prepNFB Toolbox

prepNFB

The prepNFB toolbox is a sophisticated Matlab-based solution designed to streamline the preparation and coregistration processes for OpenNFT Neurofeedback sessions. It is meticulously engineered to automate and simplify various pre-Neurofeedback steps, ensuring efficient setup for OpenNFT-based neurofeedback experiments.

At the heart of the prepNFB toolbox is a user-friendly main interface, customizable to meet the unique requirements of each neurofeedback experiment. This innovative toolbox facilitates the execution and analysis of functional localizer tasks within the same scanning session, significantly enhancing the efficiency of the initial neurofeedback training. Notably, it allows for the precise delineation and preparation of regions of interest while the participant remains positioned in the scanner, awaiting the first neurofeedback run. See video below for a demonstration of this routine.

Moreover, the prepNFB toolbox features advanced capabilities for coregistering regions of interest across neurofeedback sessions. It also enables users to project coregistration outcomes onto corresponding EPI or structural images, providing a visual quality assessment tool. This functionality is the result of an integration of original code developed by our team, alongside adapted functions from SPM.
